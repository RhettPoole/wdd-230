Boise Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

  • "Boise Chamber of Commerce"
  • This name was selected as the location is Boise, Idaho. The object of the site is to be a Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

  • The site will be used by the people of Boise, Idaho, and the Chamber of Commerce to provide updates on local issues and to provide a form submission for recommendations given by the people for how we can better our community.

Scenarios

  • Why should I move to Boise?
  • What is Boise doing about the housing increase? People from other states are moving to Boise becuase it is cheaper, but the local pay stays the same while housing prices go up. What are we doing to change this?
  • What is Boise doing to help the homeless population?
  • Idaho does not rank very high on the education leaderboard, what is Boise doing to change that?
