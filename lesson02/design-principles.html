Fitts' Law

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

lds.org

The Church of Jesus Christ's website displays a great showcase for Fitts' Law. This law is defined as the time that is required to move your fingers to place the buttons that pertain to the items that they are related to. For example the "Learn More" button is right underneath the explanation for how Jesus's gospel can bless your life.

White Space and Clean Design

Medium

medium.com

Medium's website is about 1 thing, reading and writing. Because of this, they get straight to the point. The layout is clean and easy to navigate, with a lot of white space that acts as breathing room around the elements.
